Everything You Need to Know About GSpaces Desk Setup Solutions

What is included in a complete desk setup from GSpaces?

Our complete desk setup includes everything you need for a productive workspace: premium desk/table, ergonomic chair, task lighting, cable management accessories, desk organizers, and decorative elements. Whether you need a WFH setup, office desk setup, or dream workspace, we provide a ready-to-use solution with free professional installation.

What types of desk setups does GSpaces offer?

We offer various desk setup styles to match your needs: Executive Desk Setup for professionals, Minimalist Desk Setup for clean aesthetics, Ergonomic Desk Setup for health-conscious users, WFH Setup for remote workers, Creator Desk Setup for content creators, and Luxury Desk Setup for premium spaces. All setups are customizable to your preferences.

How much does a desk setup cost at GSpaces?

Our premium desk setups start from ₹20,000 and go up to ₹1,50,000 depending on the style, materials, and accessories included. We offer complete office setup packages, WFH desk setup solutions, and dream workspace configurations at competitive prices with free installation across India.

Do you provide installation for desk setups?

Yes! Every GSpaces desk setup comes with FREE professional installation. Our team will deliver, assemble, and set up your complete workspace exactly as shown in the product images. We ensure your home office setup or office desk setup is ready to use from day one.

Is GSpaces desk setup suitable for work from home (WFH)?

Absolutely! Our WFH desk setups are specifically designed for remote professionals. We offer ergonomic solutions with proper lighting, cable management, and comfortable seating to boost productivity. Whether you need a compact home office setup or a spacious professional desk setup, GSpaces has the perfect WFH solution for you.

Can I customize my desk setup?

Yes! While our desk setups come as complete packages, we offer customization options for colors, materials, and accessories. Contact us to discuss your dream desk setup requirements, and we'll create a personalized workspace solution that matches your style and budget.

Where does GSpaces deliver desk setups?

We deliver premium desk setups across India with free installation in major cities. Whether you're setting up a home office, corporate workspace, or creative studio, GSpaces provides complete desk setup solutions with nationwide delivery and professional installation services.

What makes GSpaces desk setup different from buying furniture separately?

GSpaces offers complete, curated desk setups where every element is professionally matched for aesthetics and functionality. Instead of spending weeks researching and buying individual items, you get a ready-to-use workspace with desk, chair, lighting, and accessories that work perfectly together. Plus, we include free installation, saving you time and hassle.

How long does delivery and installation take?

Delivery typically takes 7-14 days depending on your location and the desk setup model. Installation is completed on the same day as delivery, usually within 2-3 hours. We'll schedule a convenient time for your complete office setup or WFH desk setup installation.

Do you offer warranty on desk setups?

Yes! All GSpaces desk setups come with a manufacturer's warranty on furniture items. Warranty periods vary by product (typically 1-3 years for desks and chairs). We also provide after-sales support for any issues with your home office setup or professional desk setup.
