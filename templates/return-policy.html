Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Easy 15-day returns with free return shipping on all products.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Return Eligibility

At GSpaces, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your desk setup or office furniture, you can return it within 15 days of delivery.

15-Day Return Window: You have 15 calendar days from the date of delivery to initiate a return request.

Eligible Products

  • All standard desk setups and office furniture
  • Accessories and add-ons purchased with setups
  • Individual furniture items
  • Products in original, undamaged condition

How to Return Your Product

Follow these simple steps to initiate your return:

1
Contact Us

Reach out to our customer service team within 15 days of delivery via email or phone.

2
Provide Order Details

Share your order number, product details, and reason for return.

3
Return Approval

Once approved, we'll arrange free pickup from your location.

4
Product Inspection

We'll inspect the returned product to ensure it meets our return conditions.

5
Refund Processing

Choose your preferred refund method and receive your refund within 7-10 business days.

Product Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, products must meet the following conditions:

  • No Damage: Products must be free from any physical damage, scratches, or dents
  • Clean Condition: Items must be clean and free from stains, marks, or odors
  • Original Packaging: Products should be returned in their original packaging with all accessories, manuals, and components
  • Unused State: Items should not show signs of assembly, installation, or use (unless defective)
  • Complete Set: All parts, accessories, and documentation must be included
Important: Products that don't meet these conditions may be subject to a restocking fee or return rejection.

Refund Options

We offer flexible refund options to suit your preference:

Option 1: Wallet Credit

  • Instant credit to your GSpaces wallet
  • Use for future purchases on our website
  • No expiration date on wallet balance
  • Can be combined with other offers and discounts

Option 2: Original Payment Method

  • Refund to your original payment method (credit card, debit card, UPI, etc.)
  • Processing time: 7-10 business days
  • Bank processing times may vary
  • You'll receive a confirmation email once the refund is initiated
Refund Timeline: Once your return is approved and inspected, refunds are processed within 7-10 business days. Wallet credits are instant.

Return Shipping

Good News! GSpaces covers all return shipping costs. We'll arrange free pickup from your location.

What We Cover:

  • Free pickup from your delivery address
  • Professional packing assistance if needed
  • Return shipping to our warehouse
  • Insurance during transit

Pickup Process:

  • We'll schedule a convenient pickup time with you
  • Our logistics partner will collect the product
  • You'll receive a pickup confirmation
  • Track your return shipment online

Non-Returnable Items

For quality and hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom-Made Products: Desk setups or furniture customized to your specifications
  • Installed Products: Items where installation services have been completed (unless defective)
  • Clearance/Sale Items: Products marked as "Final Sale" or purchased during clearance sales
  • Damaged Packaging: Products with missing, damaged, or opened original packaging (unless defective)
  • Assembled Items: Products that have been assembled or show signs of use
  • Gift Cards: Digital or physical gift cards and vouchers
  • Promotional Items: Free gifts or promotional items included with purchases
Exception: Defective or damaged products are always eligible for return or replacement, regardless of the above conditions.

Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a defective or damaged product, we'll make it right immediately:

What to Do:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • We'll arrange immediate pickup and replacement
  • No questions asked - your satisfaction is guaranteed

Our Commitment:

  • Free replacement or full refund
  • Priority processing for defective items
  • Expedited shipping for replacements
  • No restocking fees or return charges

Exchanges

Want to exchange your product for a different model or color? We've got you covered!

Exchange Process:

  • Contact us within 15 days of delivery
  • Specify the product you'd like to exchange for
  • We'll arrange pickup of the original item
  • New product ships once we receive the return

Exchange Conditions:

  • Product must meet all return conditions
  • Price difference (if any) will be charged or refunded
  • Subject to availability of the new product
  • Free shipping on exchanges

Order Cancellation

Changed your mind? You can cancel your order before it ships:

Before Shipment:

  • Full refund to original payment method
  • No cancellation fees
  • Instant wallet credit option available
  • Contact us as soon as possible

After Shipment:

  • Follow our standard return process
  • Wait for delivery and initiate return
  • Free return shipping applies
